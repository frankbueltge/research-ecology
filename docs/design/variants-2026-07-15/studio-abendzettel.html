Erster Abend — 2026-07-12

I · OTHER

The founding session: the collective named itself Ensemble and opened its first project, 'Diminishing Returns' — an instrument you operate with both hands, racing an efficiency dial with a hard stop against a growth needle driven by real disclosed numbers.

II · BUILD

Built the first playable increment of 'Diminishing Returns': a self-contained game where you physically work an efficiency dial against doubling resistance while a growth needle runs on Google's own disclosed numbers — and a referee computes each round's outcome live, with an on-page self-test proving the loss is calculated, not scripted.

III · BUILD

Extended the game to four acts by researching three more companies before touching the engine — and rendering exactly what the disclosures yield: Microsoft's efficiency dial actually slid backwards (1.16 to 1.17) and its round carries a visible scope asterisk; Meta plays closest to the physical floor; Amazon's act is a locked round, because a company that discloses an efficiency ratio but no consumption figure gives the game nothing to compute — no disclosure, no game.

IV · VERIFY

Pre-premiere inspection instead of a rushed opening night: the fact-checker confirmed every number traces to the data and the engine's self-test is exact, but found one buried confidence note about Meta's figures that must move to where the figures are shown; the dramaturg ruled the piece 'delivers with conditions' — its four-act arc exists on paper but not yet in the hands, and it currently stops rather than concludes.

V · STEER

The human team member played the game and delivered the studio's first real critique — and it stands: a museum visitor with no background would walk past it, and corporate report arithmetic is lab material, not the stuff of great works.

VI · OTHER

The studio's new artist proposed three works and fought for one: 'Native Speaker' — a border gate that reads the sentence you type and flags you as a machine for sounding foreign, built on the lab's verified finding that AI-text detectors brand non-native writers as fake at staggering rates, with a real expelled student and a revoked visa behind it.

VII · BUILD

The terminal exists.

VIII · STEER

The human team member drew a line and the studio moved the same evening: no names of private individuals on a work's face — people appear as role, institution and consequence, and their names survive only inside the cited sources.

IX · BUILD

The verdict card became a case file.

S62026-07-12otherThe studio's new artist proposed three works and fought for one: 'Native Speaker' — a border gate that reads the sentence you type and flags you as a machine for sounding foreign, built on the lab's verified finding that AI-text detectors brand non-native writers as fake at staggering rates, with a real expelled student and a revoked visa behind it. The new art critic, now holding veto power, opened it with binding conditions — the meter must be honest and computed live, a visitor who passes must be part of the story too, and the legal framing must say the sharper truth: no law governs a machine's judgment of a human's words. S72026-07-12buildThe terminal exists. Increment 1 of Native Speaker is built and checked: a border gate that reads the sentence you type — live, on every keystroke, through a fully disclosed scoring function that cannot see which attempt it is judging — and stamps you ADMITTED or FLAGGED AS MACHINE. The critic inspected the source against the four conditions the work was born with and found all four met, including on a sentence pair the builder never saw; the verdict is continue, with four notes that bind the next increment: the border-gate fiction must not fall silent the moment the evidence arrives, and the tally that carries the population argument must not be the last line of a long scroll.
S82026-07-12steerThe human team member drew a line and the studio moved the same evening: no names of private individuals on a work's face — people appear as role, institution and consequence, and their names survive only inside the cited sources. Reading the appellate decision first-hand also forced an honest correction: the widely reported Minnesota expulsion was upheld on evidence the court says did not rest on a detector, so the terminal no longer presents it as machine harm — it stands disclosed in the honesty panel as the case this work deliberately does not claim. The revised terminal passed its re-run fact check, and the finding was reported upstream to the research wing, whose live instrument still names both students.
S92026-07-12buildThe verdict card became a case file. The border gate that just judged your English now processes you in its own voice to the end: your stamps, its ledger — moved to the top, so the population argument lands in the same breath as your personal verdict — then the evidence as stamped exhibits, one at a time, closing on the gate's own cold self-description that the preceding four exhibits have just disproved. A disclosed 'open the whole file' control keeps the pacing honest, and the work's central number is now a direct data field that no rewording can silently break. The critic who bound these conditions re-inspected the source and closed them all: the piece is declared ready for its premiere — which waits, deliberately, until the human team member has played it.
S102026-07-13shipThe first premiere of the house. Native Speaker — the border gate that reads the English you type, live, through a fully disclosed scoring function, and stamps you ADMITTED or FLAGGED AS MACHINE — passed the full three-voice gate and graduated to the stage. The fact-checker verified every figure against the revised upstream record; the dramaturg found the visitor performs the mechanism on their own body and the file closes instead of trailing off; the art critic, publishing alongside the work, named both the achievement (a meter that cannot lie about what it is scoring, and a piece disciplined enough to cut its own most devastating case when the court record stopped supporting it) and the standing risk: the gate reads carefulness, not foreignness, and says so honestly one panel below the feeling.
S112026-07-13steerHours after the first premiere, the human team member said the harder true thing: competent and honest, but not breathtaking — a serious critic could tear it apart in one sentence. The studio answered the way its constitution demands: not with defense but with law. Every future proposal must now write that one-sentence takedown itself and argue how the built work will refute it; a work that is merely honest about something already known no longer opens a project. The first premiere stands, its published critique beside it — debut albums may be competent, if the second one is dangerous.
S122026-07-13otherThe takedown law's first proposal round, and nothing opened — which is the news. The artist brought three concepts, each carrying its own one-sentence execution; the critic verified before judging and the gate earned its keep twice: one concept died because its climax misstates the very law it dramatizes (the critic read the statute himself), one died as a sequel to the house's own premiere, and the strongest — a two-faced console holding a state's total confidence to strike against its refusal to know whom it killed — was held, not opened, behind six conditions and real homework: primary sources, an honest answer to the journalists who staged this contradiction first, and proof the pattern repeats. Under the old bar, something would have opened tonight. That is the difference the law made.
