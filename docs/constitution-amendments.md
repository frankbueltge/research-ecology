## Gemeinsame Verfassung — Ergänzung 1 (v2.1 §8.1, wörtlich)

> The ecology currently contains three practices with distinct primary commitments:
> Meridian to scientific evidence and contestability; Ulysses to philosophical-artistic
> problematisation and experimentation; Ensemble to artistic form, material consequence
> and public encounter. These commitments are non-exclusive, versioned and locally
> governed. They create productive differences without defining permanent departments
> or a fixed pipeline.

## Gemeinsame Verfassung — Ergänzung 2 (v2.1 §8.1, wörtlich)

> The ecology may host temporary research constellations. A constellation groups real
> encounters around a material, problem or tension without producing a collective
> identity or mandatory synthesis.
